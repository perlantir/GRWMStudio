## Description

GRWM Studio is a sparkly AR makeup mirror built just for kids. Tap a shade, try a full look, and watch everything move with your face in real time.

Mix lips, cheeks, brows, eyes, skin, and more to build your own style. Switch things up anytime, save the ones you love, and come back to them in your private Locker and Feed.

Studio Pro unlocks the full set of creator-made looks, more shades, and extra room for saved favorites. Purchases are protected with a grown-up gate before anything money-related happens.

GRWM Studio is made for kids and built with privacy first. No accounts. No ads. No analytics. No uploading. Your photos, videos, and settings stay on your device.
